SETTING UP THE VS
-----------------
1. Set the Startup Project as DM2122_Assignment1_181103L
2. Change the debug to x86

CONTROLS
--------
|GENERAL|
---------
1 - Enable Cull Face
2 - Disable Cull Face
3 - Fill Mode
4 - Wireframe Mode
5 - Enable Lighting
6 - Disable Lighting

----------
|MOVEMENT|
----------
Default
W - Move Forward
S - Move Backward
A - Move Left
D - Move Right
RIGHT - Look Right
LEFT - Look Left
C - Cycle Camera
E - Interact
G - Change Field
F - Toggle Torch
R - Reset Camera
NUMPAD 1 - Cam 1
NUMPAD 2 - Cam 2
NUMPAD 3 - Cam 3
NUMPAD 4 - Cam 4
NUMPAD 5 - Cam 5
NUMPAD 6 - Cam 6
NUMPAD 7 - Cam 7

Interaction
C - Shoot
Q - Quit Interaction
UP- Look Up
DOWN - Look Down
RIGHT - Look Right
LEFT - Look Left

Other Camera
C - Cycle Camera
V - Quit Camera
RIGHT - Look Right
LEFT - Look Left
G - Change Field
